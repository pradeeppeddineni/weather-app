# Privacy Policy for TempTracker India

**Last updated: February 7, 2026**

## Overview

TempTracker India is a weather tracking application that displays temperature and weather data for cities across India. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

TempTracker India does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

- **No account required** — the app does not require sign-in or registration
- **No personal information collected** — we do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers
- **No location tracking** — the app does not access your device's GPS or location services
- **No analytics or tracking** — we do not use any analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, or advertising frameworks
- **No cookies or local tracking** — the app does not use cookies for tracking purposes

## Data Usage

The app fetches publicly available weather data from the Open-Meteo API (https://open-meteo.com) to display temperature and weather information. This data is:

- Fetched directly from the Open-Meteo public API
- Not stored on any server we operate
- Cached locally on your device only for offline access and performance

## Third-Party Services

- **Open-Meteo API**: Weather data provider. Their privacy policy is available at https://open-meteo.com/en/terms

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
